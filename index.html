Our Story

A Space Where Art Lives and Collectors Thrive

Luminary Gallery was founded on the belief that exceptional art should find exceptional homes. Since our founding, we have championed both emerging voices and established masters, building a collection that spans genres, periods, and perspectives.

We believe in the intimacy of the collector-artist relationship. Every pre-order through our platform is handled personally — we guide you through condition reports, provenance documentation, insurance, and delivery.
